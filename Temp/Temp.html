Latitude vs. Max Temperature

Analyzing Latitude against shows a distinct pattern that closer one gets to the equator (x-axis: 0), the hotter the temperature gets. Inversely, further away one gets from the equator, the colder it gets. Hence, creating an arch pattern with the apex seemingly near the equator.

While there seems to be a consistentcy of dtata points from the southern hemisphere (less than x-axis: 0), this is likely due to less land mass and cities below the equator.
